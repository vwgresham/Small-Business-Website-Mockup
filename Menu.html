breads

We sell breads in a variety of shapes including as baguettes, table loaves, pan loaves, sandwich roles and dinner roles. Here are our doughs:

  • Black Olive a moist, chewy bread filled with black olives
  • Country Sourdough a classic sourdough filled with flavor
  • Country White a classic, mild white with a thick crust
  • Double Seeded Wheat an earthy whole wheat bread with a seeded crust
  • Potato Onion Dill a moist bread flavored robutly with onions and dill
  • Pumpernickel a traditional pumpernickel speckled with sunflower seeds and currants
  • Rosemary a delicious sourdough made with olive oil and rosemary
  • Rustic Italian an airy ciabatta with a chewy crust
  • Rye with Caraway a robust rye with a tangy twist
  • Whole Wheat Oat Pecan a sweet whole wheat bread studded with nuts
