about

Lily's Bakery is committed to creating quality breads and pastries while maintaing the art of traditional hand-crafting. Ingredients are chosen with emphasis on the team's high standards of organic and ethical qualities. Lily's Bakery attempts to maintain a calm, relaxing setting as well as offering capacities for gathering. In addition to artisanal breads and pastries, Lilys' Bakery also offers a variety of coffees and espresso drinks.

history

Lily attended Barnard College in Morningside Heights, earning her degree in Economics. After graduation, she prolonged graduate school, instead working a variety of internships and traveling. During this time, she developed her love of baking and a dream to open her own bakery.

So, Lily went back to school, taking classes in marketing and business. A few years and lots of coins in the "Savings" jar, she finally opened the bakery in 2006. Since its opening, Lily's Bakery has been joined by Mark, who contributed a more formal background in bread-making as well as a French twist.
