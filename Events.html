services

Though we do not provide staffing for any events, we can deliver hot food for a variety of events. We often cater picnics, brunches, and even business meetings. In addition, we can assemble gift and welcome baskets full of delicious goodies.Not sure if we've got you covered? Feel free to inquire on our contact page. We're happy to work with you on your vision.

pricing prices include delivery

Every event is unique and we often work in varying degrees of involvement but here are a few starting points:

  • pastry platters a selection of breakfast pastries served with butter and jams. from $6 a person.
  • quiches individual quiches in customizable flavor choices. from $8 a person.
  • sandwiches your choice of a variety of fresh vegetales and meats on our signature breads. from $11 a person.
  • dessert platters a selection of our delectable desserts. from $5 a person.
  • custom cakes a custom cake for any occasion. price upon consultation.
